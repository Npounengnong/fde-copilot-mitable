# Communication Context — Acme Bakery

## With Riya (Owner)
Lead with the customer's experience, not metrics. "We're saving the late-night customers a wait" lands better than "deflection rate is 59%." She still appreciates numbers but in the second paragraph, not the first.

Don't propose features. Describe a customer pain point, then ask if she's seeing it.

Avoid the word "deflection" — sounds dismissive of customers. Say "resolved by AI" instead.

## Sensitive topic — Voice / phone support
She has explicitly said phone stays human-only. Don't propose voice AI even when it would obviously help. See DR-002.

## Channel preference
Text > email > phone. Phone only for incidents.

## Cadence
Monthly 30-min sync. No QBR formality. Keep updates terse.
